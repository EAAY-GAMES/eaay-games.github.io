Privacy Policy

Last Updated: March 17, 2026

VaultEx is committed to protecting your privacy. This policy explains how your data is processed when you use our application.

1. Data Collection & Local Storage

VaultEx is a "Local-First" application. Photos, videos, and documents you add to your vault are encrypted and stored locally on your device. We (the developers) do not have access to these files and they are NOT uploaded to our servers.

2. Third-Party Services

We use the following third-party services to ensure app functionality and provide advertisements:

  • Google Play Services / AdMob: Used for in-app advertisements. Device identifiers may be used for ad personalization.
  • Firebase (Google): Used for authentication, cloud settings sync (optional), and app performance analysis.

3. User Rights

Users can permanently delete their account and all local data at any time via the "Settings" menu. Once deleted, data cannot be recovered.

4. Contact

If you have any questions, please contact us at: eaay.games@gmail.com
